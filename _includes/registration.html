Registration Fees

All the participants must pay the conference registration fee.

Registration Type Before 15th May 2022 After 15th May 2022
Non-student £280 £340
Student £180 £220
Tutorials (add-on) £60 £70

Registration grants access to all sessions during the conference.

All papers accepted for the conference must be presented by an author/co-author. All authors/co-authors registered for the conference are entitled to present two papers. For any further paper (i.e., third, fourth, etc.) presented by the registered author/co-author, a mandatory fee of USD $150 (GBP £115) per additional paper must be paid.

The tutorial fee applies on a ‘per-tutorial’ basis. You can see the list of available tutorials here.
